Biography

I am currently a Senior Engineer at Baidu. Before that I obtained Ph.D. degree from Southeast University in 2023. My research interests includes computer vision and deep learning, with a particular emphasis on 2D/3D human pose estimation and autonomous driving. I am dedicated to developing cutting-edge solutions for real-world computer vision applications.
